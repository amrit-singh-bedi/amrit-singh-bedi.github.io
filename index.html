Thank you for visiting my webpage.

I am an Assistant Professor in the Computer Science department, with a joint appointment in the ECE department, at the University of Central Florida, USA. Before joining UCF, I was an Assistant Research Professor/Scientist at the University of Maryland, College Park, where I worked closely with Prof. Dinesh Manocha, Prof. Pratap Tokekar, and Prof. Furong Huang.

Prior to my time at UMD, I worked with Dr. Alec Koppel and Dr. Brian Sadler at the U.S. Army Research Laboratory, where I gained valuable experience in real-world applications of machine learning and AI. I earned my Ph.D. in Electrical Engineering from the Indian Institute of Technology Kanpur under the supervision of Prof. Ketan Rajawat, where my thesis focused on distributed and online learning with stochastic gradient methods.

Research

My research focuses on machine learning and artificial intelligence, with an emphasis on trustworthy, adaptive, and efficient methods. I am particularly interested in advancing the theoretical foundations and practical algorithms needed for safe and reliable AI systems.

  • AI Alignment: theoretical insights and practical algorithms
  • Reinforcement Learning from Human Feedback
  • Safety and trustworthiness in generative AI
  • Optimization techniques for the AI era
  • Data efficiency in machine learning
